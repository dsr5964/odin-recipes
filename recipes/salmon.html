Salmon

Description

Salmon is a very easy fish dish to make. It can be modified in many different ways and it is not too heavy. It is a great source of protein especially when you don't want to eat red meat or chicken. You can add many garnishes and sides to add more to your salmon fish.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons light olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon freshley ground black pepper or to taste
  • 2 fillets salmon

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, basil, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets in a small glass or ceramic baking dish; pour marinade over salmon. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for about 1 hour, turning occasionally.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Transfer salmon fillets onto a large piece of aluminum foil. Spoon marinade on top and fold up the foil to seal. Place sealed foil packs on baking sheet.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
