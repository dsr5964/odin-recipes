Chicken Fajitas

Description

Chicken fajitas are an easy classic Mexican dish and one of my favorites to make. It provides a flavorful meal and it very healthy. It is important to season the chicken and tossing it with vibrant vegetables. It is perfect when wrapped with tortillas and toppings as well. My personal favorite toppings are sour cream, guacamole, and salsa, but feel free to customize the fajitas based on personal preference.

The fajitas are not only delicious and flavorful but they are fun for the entire family. The freedom of choice is what makes them fun as everyone has their own spin on the recipe. You can add peppers, onions, cheese, etc. You can even change out the meat for beef as well. Have fun making this great recipe for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Bell Peppers: red, yellow, green
  • Onion
  • Tortillas
  • Olive oil
  • Lime
  • Fajita seasoning: can be pre-made packets or your own homemade seasoning
  • Optional: sour gream, pico de Gallo, avocados, salsa

Steps

  1. Season the chicken. Sprinkle the fajita seasoning on both sides of the chicken.
  2. Cook the chicken. Coat the skillet with oil and cook the chicken breasts on each side until there is no pink, typically 7-8 minutes. Then remove fgrom the pan and let it rest before cutting into strips.
  3. Saute the bell peppers and onion. While the chicken cooks, start slicing the bell peppers and onions. Then saute them in that skillet for a few minutes until caramelized.
  4. Mix all the ingredients together and serve. Slice the chicken into thin strips and add back to skillet. Add a squeeze of fresh lime juice on top, and stir everything together before serving.
  5. Serve the fajitas. You can serve it in tortillas and customize it with your favorite toppings!
